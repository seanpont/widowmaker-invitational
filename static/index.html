About The Widowmaker Invitational

The Widowmaker is more than a rock-strewn, washed-out gully running up the backbone of Mount Tamalpais. It's a Strava segment, a threat towards tired legs, a ravine so steep that only goats may pass.

On July 26th 2015, a non-organized unofficial run occurred up said ravine to the summit, after which prizes were awarded non-randomly.

Due to the narrowness of the course, as well as restrictions on the number of Galens allowed on the mountain at a time, this event was by invitation only.

Awards

The top male and female runners were each awarded a goat.

Since most people have small apartments and non-agricultural jobs, the award was mostly symbolic. After the event, the goats were returned to the ranch and visitation rights instated.
